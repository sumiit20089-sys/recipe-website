Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 10, 2026

Welcome to RecipeNest ("we", "our", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect information on our website, recipenest.com, and applies to users in the United States and Canada.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when using our services:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, and subject/message details when you fill out our Contact Form or subscribe to our Newsletter.
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect information about how you interact with our website, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent, and referral URLs.
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies to improve browser speed, remember session preferences, and analyze site performance. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of our site may not function properly.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To deliver recipes, meal plans, and newsletter updates.
  • To respond to your inquiries, customer service requests, or feedback.
  • To improve website design, functionality, and performance.
  • To protect the security of our site and comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing and Disclosing Information

RecipeNest does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share your data only under these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party providers who assist us in operating our website, managing newsletters (e.g., email marketing platforms), or analyzing usage.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, subpoena, or to protect the safety, rights, or property of RecipeNest, our users, or others.

4. Compliance for US Residents

Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)

Our website is designed for individuals aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover a child under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete it immediately.

California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)

California residents have the right to request access to the specific pieces of personal information we have collected, request deletion of their data, and opt out of any data sharing. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@recipenest.com.

5. Compliance for Canadian Residents

Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA)

We comply with PIPEDA principles regarding consent, purpose limitation, accuracy, safeguards, and individual access. By using our website, you consent to the collection and processing of your personal information as detailed in this policy. You have the right to challenge the accuracy and completeness of your data and have it amended or deleted.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard administrative, technical, and physical security measures (including SSL encryption) to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. However, no data transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@recipenest.com
